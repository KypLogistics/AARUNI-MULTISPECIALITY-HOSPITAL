Orthopedics & Sports Medicine

Department of Orthopedics at Aaruni Hospital is equipped with best of infrastructure & modern technology to treat every regular and severe orthopedic related condition with utmost diligence & care.

Sports Medicine is a part of Department of Orthopedics at Aaruni Hospital that deals with sports injuries. It specialises in keyhole surgery of knee, shoulder, hip, elbow, wrist and ankle, and physiotherapy & rehabilitation besides other related regular procedures and treatments.

Advanced Services

  • Total Knee Replacement
  • Total Hip Replacement
  • Revision Hip Replacement
  • Shoulder Replacement
  • Elbow Arthroscopy
  • Tumour Limb Saving Surgery
  • Pelvic Osteotomy Surgery
  • Pelvic Acetabular Surgery
  • Paediatric Orthopaedic Surgery (Club foot, limb length differences, gait abnormalities (limping), fractures, bone or joint infections and tumours, epiphyseal injuries, hip diseases like perthes and slipped femoral epiphysis)
  • Orthopaedic Oncology
  • Joint replacements
  • Total and Reverse Total Shoulder Replacements
  • Unicompartmental Knee Replacements
  • Elbow replacement
  • Deformity correction
  • Specialised joint care (elbow, shoulder, knee, hip, ankle)

Equipped to Care

  • Intra-operative image guidance system
  • Physiotherapy and rehabilitation including Computer Assisted Rehabilitation & Laser Therapy
  • 24 hour trauma care and CT scan services
