Department of General Surgery & Laparoscopic

General Surgery Department at Aaruni Hospital has highly experienced competent team of Surgeons.
Our surgeons help in making the surgical experience easier and stress-free for patients.

Key Procedures

  • GI Surgery & Laparoscopic Surgery
  • Laparoscopic Appendectomy
  • Bariatric Surgery including Sleeve Gastrectomy
  • Gastric Bypass Surgery
  • Diagnostic Laparoscopy
  • General Surgery including cyst removals, incision & drainage of abscesses
  • Abdominal Surgery
  • Advanced Haemorrhoidal Surgery
  • Advanced Hernia Repair & Hydrocele Surgery (adult and paediatric)
  • Gallbladder Surgery

The general surgery department often collaborates with other related departments for performing surgeries that need more than one kind of specialists. General surgeons work with specialists in-

  • Hepato-biliary
  • Pancreatic and Colorectal Surgery
  • Venaes and Arterial Surgery
  • Breast and Endocrine Surgery

Laparoscopic Surgical Techniques

The general surgeons at Aaruni Hospital also use Laparoscopic surgical techniques whenever required and possible. The laparoscopic procedures redece post-operative discomfort, hellp recover quickly, shorter hospital stay-less blood loss and minimalise scars.
